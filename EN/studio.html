ANKAMA

Created in 2001, Ankama is now one of the most recognized studios in the world of video games. Ankama is a true transmedia group, with a solid anchor in video games, but also in animation, publishing and publishing board games.

Accidental Queens

Independent French video game development studio, founded by three women from the video game industry, based in Marcq-En-Baroeul. Seeking to create games that integrate new mechanics and explore subjects of daily life as well as social issues, through innovative storytelling tools.

Couch Game Crafters

Independent studio based in Hazebrouck. Its main goal is to create fun gaming experiences to meet up with friends.

CCCP

CCCP is a studio for the development of independent games and serious games based in France, with more than 14 years of existence. Specialized in health projects and support for serious games projects, from their genesis to their completion, whether with associations, SMEs or large international groups.

Ironequal

Founded in 2017, Ironequal is a video game development and publishing studio. He develops interactive experiences on different platforms: PC, mobile, consoles but also in virtual and augmented reality.

Kluster Games

Founded on August 11, 2009, Kluster Games is an independent game studio in which five friends have come together to create old-fashioned games that everyone can enjoy. Based in Lille, their first game “Struggle For Light” was released on itch.io on May 25, 2018 and on steam on July 7, 2018.

Too Kind studios

Took Kind Studios is a video game creation studio based in the Plaine Images ecosystem, in Tourcoing. Since its foundation in 2015, the studio has aimed to produce innovative narrative and graphic games, to put an end to the usual stereotypes and stereotypes of video games.

Klondike

Behind the Klondike collective hides a group of friends passionate about creating video games who have been offering highly colorful video games to the public since 2012.

Augmenteo

Founded in 2017 by enthusiasts of new technologies, Augmenteo is a creative studio that designs and develops fun and innovative activities. Their goal is to democratize and simplify access to augmented reality for creators of content such as tourist sites, event agencies or museums.

VERTICAL

Vertical is a creative studio born in 2012 from the desire of its founders to produce quality projects with a small team of extremely competent enthusiasts. In 2019, Vertical joined the Celsius Online group.

1P2P

Independent studio founded in 2018 by two alumni from Ankama and Hydravision, 1P2P offers independent video games for consoles and PC. With more than ten years of experience in the video game field, the studio specializes in game design, development and animation.

Aardwolf Interactive

Created in 2019, Aardwolf Interactive is an independent video game development studio based in Amiens. They specialize in the action adventure PC game rich in dialogue and combat.

Mighty Rocket Studio

Founded in 2013, Mighty Rocket Studio is a video game development studio for consoles and PC based in Tourcoing, created by the former founders of Hydravision Entertainment.
